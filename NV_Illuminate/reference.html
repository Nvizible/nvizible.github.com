IL_VectorOp
The VectorOp node will perform an operation on a vector, or a pair of vectors.

IL_RelightPrep
The RelightPrep node will prepare extra vectors that may be needed by other nodes.

IL_Light
The Light node will generate lighting passes based on the surfaces and lights that can be defined in Nukeâ€™s 3D space.

IL_PointCloud
The PointCloud node will generate a point cloud in 3D space from P and colour image data.

IL_PositionMatte
The PositionMatte node generates a matte based on point positions. It can be a bubble around a point, a gradient between two points or a distance from a point.

IL_VectorEnvironment
The VectorEnvironment node will map a vector to an environment mapping. Most commonly used with a Reflect vector to simulate environment reflections.
